Study of radiation

The Federal Office for Radiation Protection Bundesamt für Strahlenschutz (BfS) responsible for the large-scale investigation of environmental radioactivity and for the detection of radioactive sources from the air. There are two airworthy standby gamma spectrometric measuring systems at each of the BfS sites in Berlin and Munich in order to fulfill this task. The measuring systems of the BfS are scaffolded in helicopters of the federal police or civil protection.

On behalf of the state Baden-Württemberg, the Federal Office for Radiation Protection and the Federal Police carried out a joint exercise to detect and assess environmental radioactivity based on helicopter-borne measurements from 11 to 14 April 2011.
